# 🏬 Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard
### 1. Project Title / Headline
### 📊 Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard
An interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, product categories, and regional trends across a Superstore business in the USA.   

### 2. Short Description / Purpose
The Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of business performance by analyzing sales, profit margins, return rates, product categories, customer segments, and geographic trends. The dashboard helps business managers and decision-makers identify growth opportunities, optimize product strategies, and improve overall business performance.   

### 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:   
• 📊 Tableau Desktop – Primary data visualization platform used to build interactive dashboards.   
• 📂 Data Preparation – Data cleaning and transformation performed before visualization.   
• 📈 Calculated Fields & Parameters – Used for dynamic metrics, filters, and user interactions.   
• 🗺️ Geospatial Mapping – Used to visualize sales performance across different states in the USA.   
• 📊 Interactive Dashboards – Filters, KPI Cards, Maps, Trend Analysis, and Comparative Visualizations.   
• 📁 File Format – .twb/.twbx for development and .png for dashboard previews.   

### 4. Data Source
Source: Sample Superstore Dataset   
The dataset contains retail transaction records from a Superstore business operating across multiple regions of the United States.   
Key attributes include:   
• Order Date   
• Sales   
• Profit   
• Product Category   
• Sub-Category   
• Customer Segment   
• State   
• Region   
• Returns   
• Product Information   
The data enables comprehensive analysis of sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior.   

### 5. Features / Highlights
Business Problem:   
Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional data every day. However, identifying profitable products, understanding customer behavior, and tracking regional performance can be challenging without effective visualization tools.   
Key questions include:   
• Which product categories generate the highest sales?   
• Which states contribute the most revenue?   
• Which customer segments drive business growth?   
• How have sales changed over time?   
• What is the current profit margin and return rate?   
Answering these questions manually is difficult and time-consuming.   

Goal of the Dashboard   
• Monitor overall business performance.   
• Analyze sales and profit trends.   
• Compare category and sub-category performance.   
• Evaluate customer segments and regional sales distribution.   
• Support data-driven business decisions.   

Walkthrough of Key Visuals   
📌 KPI Cards   
The dashboard provides key business performance indicators:   
• Total Sales: ₹2.30M   
• Profit Margin: 12.47%   
• Return Rate: 5.91%   
These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business health.   

📊 Sales by Category   
Displays sales contribution across major categories:   
• Technology   
• Furniture   
• Office Supplies   
Helps identify the most revenue-generating product categories.   

📈 Sales by Sub-Category   
Ranks product sub-categories based on sales performance.   
Examples include:   
• Phones   
• Chairs   
• Storage   
• Tables   
• Binders   
• Machines   
This visual helps identify high-performing products and business opportunities.   

🗺️ Sales by States   
An interactive geographic map displaying sales distribution across different states in the USA.   
Users can quickly identify top-performing regions and geographic sales patterns.   

👥 Sales by Segment   
A donut chart showing sales contribution from different customer segments:   
• Consumer   
• Corporate   
• Home Office   
Helps businesses understand customer distribution and target key segments effectively.   

📉 Sales Trend Analysis   
A time-series visualization displaying monthly sales trends from 2014 to 2018.   
This helps identify:   
• Growth patterns   
• Seasonal fluctuations   
• Long-term business performance

This visual helps identify best-selling products and inventory priorities.
